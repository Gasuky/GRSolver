## Granted Patents

### GB2615187.8 — CNT-NEMS Acoustic Switch Architecture
**UK Intellectual Property Office**

Carbon nanotube nanoelectromechanical switches activated by bulk acoustic waves  
propagating through a silicon substrate via an aluminium nitride piezoelectric layer.  
Designed for zero-leakage power gating in integrated circuits, with ongoing  
exploration into physical hardware identity applications.

---

### GB2614957.5 — Molecular Dielectric Interface for Piezoelectric-Semiconductor Integration
**UK Intellectual Property Office**

A self-assembled monolayer derived from functionalised perfluorinated compounds,  
positioned between a crystalline alpha-quartz substrate and an active silicon logic layer.  
Provides simultaneous electrical isolation, thermal protection, and lossless acoustic  
transmission of piezoelectric clock signals into the transistor matrix.
