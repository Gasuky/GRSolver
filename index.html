Projects

CNT-NEMS Acoustic PUF — Hardware Authentication

Investigating CNT-NEMS resonator arrays as physically unclonable functions for cyber-physical authentication. Each switch carries a unique mechanical resonance fingerprint determined by atomic-scale fabrication variation — physically unclonable and tamper-evident by construction. Proposal submitted to ARIA Trust Everything, Everywhere 2026. Background IP: Patent GB2615187.8 — CNT-NEMS switch architecture with bulk acoustic wave activation via AlN piezoelectric substrate (UK IPO).

Originally designed for zero-leakage power gating in integrated circuits. Currently being explored as a physical unclonable function architecture for cyber-physical hardware authentication. #### GB2614957.5 — Molecular Dielectric Interface for Piezoelectric-Semiconductor Integration UK Intellectual Property Office A self-assembled monolayer derived from functionalised perfluorinated compounds, deposited between a crystalline alpha-quartz substrate and an active silicon logic layer.
